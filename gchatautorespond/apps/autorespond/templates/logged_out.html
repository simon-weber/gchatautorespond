Autoresponder

for Google Hangouts


Autoresponder lets you reply automatically to Google Hangouts messages, even if you're offline.
Due to Google's impending shutdown of Hangouts, the service is no longer supported.
It will run until Hangouts is fully shut down, but there's no guarantee that it works.

out of office / vacation reply for chat

Going away? Gmail can respond automatically to your emails, but not your chat messages. Autoresponder can fill the gap.

avoid missing chat messages to unmonitored accounts

Have accounts that foward email that you never log into? You could be missing chat messages. Autoresponder can tell people how to get in touch and let you know about incoming messages.

automatically direct people to a chat platform you're migrating to

Switching from Google Hangouts to something like Slack? Autoresponder can tell existing contacts where to find you.

Features

multiple account support

Want autoreplies on multiple accounts? No problem; you can attach them all to one Autoresponder account.

optional email notifications

You can receive emails when messages are received so you don't miss a thing.

secure OAuth 2.0 access

Autoresponder can only access your chat messages, and will never use them for anything other than sending replies.

automatic status detection

Normal conversations won't be interrupted: autoresponses are only sent when you're offline or away.

custom rate limits and user settings

Autoresponses can be configured to fire as often as you'd like and even handle contacts specific differently.

open source and self-hostable

Contributions welcome! And if you have your own server, feel free to self-host.
